About

My name is Adrian S Roman. My research interests lie in the intersection of mathematics, software engineering and audio signal processing.

Research & Engineering

In addition to my research and studies, I am currently employed as an Audio Software Engineer at Tesla. My responsibilities at Tesla include developing audio signal processing algorithms, machine learning models for audio applications, infotainment UI, firmware, validation pipelines, and various other tasks related to Linux machines.

Prior to Tesla, I gained valuable experience as an intern at Oscillo Biosciences. During my time there, I designed and implemented biophysical dynamical models inspired by neuroscience research in various products. One notable achievement includes the development of an iOS app that utilizes a non-linear oscillator to provide a digital therapy for patients with language disfluencies. The app's core algorithm adaptively synchronizes to the tapping rhythm of the user on a device screen, effectively mimicking the rhythmic interactions of a human therapist to improve synchronization skills.
