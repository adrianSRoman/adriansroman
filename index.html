About

My name is Adrian S Roman. I am double majoring in Computer Science and Applied Mathematics at UC Davis. My research interests lie in the intersection between mathematics and computer science. In my interdisciplinary work, I try to leverage machine intelligence from biophysical and mathematical foundations.

Research & Engineering

The research and engineering projects I have developed involve mathematical modeling in neouroscience. I have a passion for bringing nonlinear dynamical systems to life in engineering applications. For instance, I have created an iOS app which aims to provide a digital therapy for training synchronization on patients with language disfluencies. The app's core algorithm is an non-linear oscillator that adaptively synchronizes to the tapping rhythm of patients on a device screen. Thus, the app mimics the rhythmic interactions of a human therapists.
